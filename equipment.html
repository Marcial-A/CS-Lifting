Intimidated by all the various equipment at the gym?

We're here to show you how to use each piece of equipment properly!

Name: Ez Curl Bar

Use: Can be used in multiple ways. Bicep Curls/Preacher Curl, Skull-crushers(Lying down on a bench with the bar moving from just above your head to an extended and raised position. Or could be used for wrist curls-- where you let the barbell and hand hang from your knees and proceed to curl with your wrists.
